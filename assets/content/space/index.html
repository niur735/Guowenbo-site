Title: Talking Smoking

Cigarette butts, Cigarette Packages, Spring 2018

Statement:

Title: Hole

Performance, Canvas, Threads, Scissors, 2017 Spring

Statement:
[Cutting the thread with scissors from the back of the canvas, try to expose the mouth and say “I am”]
In the modern world, people are always trying to know about some people with the information provided by their relationships with others. The relationship between these people and others are just like the obvious black threads on a white canvas; however, is understanding a person through such “obvious” information correct?

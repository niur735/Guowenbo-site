Distance can always form to expel the third person in many relations; the distance is not only about left and right, but also inside and outside, reality and fakeness on canvas.
In my eyes, the whole world is formed by structures. The black and white lines are languages that human talk to the unconscious items and also the items’ response to human behaviors. Each drawing in this set stands for an object or a phenomenon. As a method to question and analyze the differences between different persons’ acceptance of visible information, the set of black and white line drawings is where I start my artistic researches.
